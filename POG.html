      4.5
      Buffets are about eating as much as you want & Pirates Of Grill is one such place. In my friend circle it is famous for non-veg buffet but being a pure-veg person i found this place good for veggie's too, may be because of Navratra's season. The ambience is great, one can relax and can enjoy the food here. The starters are amazing and one's stomach might get filled even with them because the serving staff will ask you again and again to have them, among the starters crispy corns, salsa potato, paneer and grilled pineapple were the best. I deducted 0.5 for the main course because i think some improvement needs to be done in it and also the serving staff didn't served the softdrinks even after telling them 3-4 times because their manager didn't told them to. In the end, there is a wide variety in the mouth-watering deserts. Overall it was an amazing experience, i along with my friends enjoyed a lot.
